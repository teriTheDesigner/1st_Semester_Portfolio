About me

Who am I?

I am a 26 year old Slovak and have been living in DK for the past 7 years. I am definitely not a morning person, I usually get stuff done when everyone is sleeping.

I have always had a passion for art, I am really loving watercolours at the moment. I enjoy playing Apex Legends, taking care of my plants and creating different DIYs. My perfect day would be playing in the snow for hours and drinking hot cacao (+whipped cream of course).

I will never say no to a chocolate cake and a nice chat :)
