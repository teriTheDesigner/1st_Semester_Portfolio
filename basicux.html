Theme 03: Basic UX

03.01.01 - Data collection

In the Data Collection project, each student had to make their own research (both primary and secondary) about the given topic.

Desk research was used to get an overview about a subject in general, to find some interesting insights which can become building blocks for further research.

With the data from field observation, survey and interviews, I was able to find common features and use the results in the next assignment.

03.02.01 - Early prototypes

Since this was a group project, we needed to bring together our research analysis and come up with an idea. The crazy 8's method helped us with the ideation process and we used the dot voting to choose the best idea.

When creating the UX storyboard, we explored the user journey from recognizing the problem, navigating on the website to actually buying our product.

After we had a clear idea, we were ready to make a paper prototype which we tested using think aloud test, to high fidelity interactive prototype in Adobe XD.

03.03.01 - Solution

As you can see from the pictures, our finished product was made in minimalistic design and we focused on the usability of the website. Using simple pictures and icons makes it easier to see what the page is about and simplifies the shopping process.

Followin the basic design conventions, the form only has the necessary information with a low opacity text to help users fill it out. When they make a mistake, the specific problem will be shown in red so they know exactly what they need to fix to continue with the purchase.
