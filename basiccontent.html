Theme 05: Basic Content

05.01.01 - A story without words

For this short video project, I decided to record myself watering plants. At the beginning, I am showing the plants with dropping leaves so the viewer will understand that something is wrong.

To enhance the atmoshpere, I color corrected the video by lowering saturation with adjustment layers. Since there is a lot of greenery shown in the video, the intro part creates a significant contrast and helps to express the point of the story.

Because I had a lot of footage, I had to prioritize and cut the video to fit the frame of 1 minute. To fit in all the shots I liked, I adjusted the speed which also made the video more dynamic.

05.02.01 - Pre-production part 1: Treatment

In this group assignment, we had to decide on the basics for our video. We went through many different ideas and discussed which parts we liked and which were not so good. At the end, we came with the theme of `Coming together for good times.` representing people from three different target groups.

Writing down a Logline and The treatment helped us to stay on the same page throughout the whole shooting process, and it was something we could always come back to.

05.02.02 - Pre-production part 2: Storyboard

Because we had a clear idea written in Logline and Treatment, it was easier to plan the shooting process in a storyboard. The challenging part was deciding on the lenght of separate clips to make sure that the intro will not be too long, but will show everything the viewer needs to know to understand the story.

In the story, we are following three personas with different backgrounds so we decided to use split screen and show them all at the same time. We planned to make some medium shots as well as some close-ups, for example how the personas are walking or beer being poured to glasses.

To capture the atmoshpere of the beer walk it was important to use different camera angles and compositions. We decided to play music in the video, which is why we did not have to worry about diegetic sounds when shooting.

05.02.03 - Customer pitch

For this project, we decided to create a completely new visual idedntity for the company. Based on desk research, observation and interviews, we created three different target groups, which fit into the company's wish of attracting younger audience.

We decided to adjust the colour scheme and added different graphic elements to all parts of the website. The banner at the loading page gives the users a taste of the whole experience and makes them curious to read more.

Adding a simple game to the website is another way of getting an attention of younger audience and making them stay longer on the website. After they win the game, they are redirected to the booking page, to increase the amount of sales.
